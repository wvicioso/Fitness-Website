How Do I Gym?

To gym correctly, you first need to understand how the muscles of your body work. Its actually pretty simple. All of your muscles make one of two possible motions. They either PUSH or PULL.

Muscles That Push

  • Chest
  • Shoulders
  • Triceps

Muscles That Pull

  • Biceps
  • Back
  • Forearms

The idea is to work out groups of muscles together. Do pulling exercises one day

What about Legs?

Abs?
